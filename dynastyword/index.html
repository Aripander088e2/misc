This is a quick-and-dirty implementation of orthographic 余計文字クロス, but with letters and numbers rather than japanese characters. A converter from penpa is here. The rules are:
  1. Blacken some squares. Do not blacken any two adjacent squares, and the unblackened squares must form a connected region. (These are dynasty rules.)
  2. "Words" (contiguous sequences of more than one unblackened square, across or down) cannot start with a digit, and if a word has two adjacent digits the first must always be strictly less than the second.
  3. No word can appear more than once in the grid.
